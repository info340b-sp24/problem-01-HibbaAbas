Hari Seldon

Hari Seldon is from Issac Asimov's Foundation series. He was the creater of psychohistory; a new scientific field that combines history, sociology, and math to make predictions about the future of large inertias. He predicted the fall of the Galactic empire and set a complex plan in motion to reduce the following dark ages significantly.
